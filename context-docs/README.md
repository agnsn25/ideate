# Context Documents

Place your research docs here to enrich idea generation and validation.

## What to add
- AI industry reports (State of AI, a16z market maps)
- Your own notes on gaps you've observed
- Recent funding round summaries
- "Ideas I wish existed" notes
- Regulatory landscape summaries for verticals you care about

## Supported formats
- Markdown (.md)
- Plain text (.txt)

The more opinionated your context docs, the better the outputs.
